What We Do

VertNet is a network of vertical farms located around the the world in both rural and urban areas. An efficient delivery system combined with a blockchain network ensures that all crops get delivered to all users with full transparancy.

Efficient Vertical Farms.

A combination of refrigerator-sized Aeroponic vertical farms that fit neatly into apartment and houses and container-sized fatms provide a reliable source of food fo urban areas.
